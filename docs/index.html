Methodology

All data sourced from Wikipedia election pages and the Hungarian National Election Office (NVI). 2026 results at 98.78% reporting.

The Gallagher index (Least Squares index) is calculated as: LSq = √(½ × Σ(vᵢ − sᵢ)²), where vᵢ is vote share and sᵢ is seat share for party i.

Proportional seat allocation uses simple quota (vote% × total seats), rounded. German MMP simulation assumes overhang seats are kept.

County-level constituency breakdowns for 2026 are estimated where individual OEVK data was unavailable. All estimates are flagged.

Built with D3.js. Data pipeline: Python + SQLite. Analysis: 4 hypotheses tested against 10 elections (1990–2026).
